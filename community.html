The Standard

How we run the room.

The quality of a community is set by its rules — and more importantly, by whether anyone enforces them. We do.

01
No Bad Beat Posts

We don't post hands to vent — we post them to learn. Every hand shared should come with a question or a specific spot you want analyzed.

02
No Ego

Leave the results-oriented thinking at the door. We talk about process, ranges, and decisions — not how big you run or who got lucky.

03
Stay Engaged

Inactive members get removed. This community grows through participation — if you go quiet for too long, your seat goes to someone who wants it.

04
Give Back

Everyone here was a beginner at something. If you know something another member doesn't — share it. The room gets better when everyone contributes.

05
Respect the Process

Poker is a long game. We don't panic over downswings or celebrate upswings. We study, we adjust, we trust the process.

06
Show Up

Weekly study sessions are where the real growth happens. Attendance isn't mandatory — but the members who show up consistently are the ones who improve the fastest.
